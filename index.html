About The Doctor

Dr. Seema Sachan

With over 20 years of experience in anatomic pathology, encompassing histopathology, cytopathology and immunohistochemistry, Dr. Seema Sachan is a trusted name in diagnostic pathology.

She has worked at high-volume tertiary care centers such as BLK Max Superspeciality Hospital, New Delhi, and Paras Hospitals Lab, Gurgaon, with a special focus on surgical oncology.

She has been an active member of multidisciplinary tumor boards, regularly discussing complex oncological cases and contributing to evidence-based patient management decisions.

Education & Training

  • SENIOR RESIDENCY: PGIMER, Chandigarh.
